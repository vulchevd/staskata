One Bedroom Apartment

Our elegant One Bedroom Apartment spans **65 square meters**, designed for comfort and style. Perfect for individuals or couples seeking a bit more room, this apartment combines functionality and elegance in a way that makes everyday living enjoyable and stress-free.
