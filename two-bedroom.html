Two-Bedroom Apartment

Our two-bedroom apartments offer spacious, comfortable living for families or individuals needing extra space. With a total area of 84 sq.m, each unit includes:

  • A large living room with dining area and open-plan kitchen
  • Two bright bedrooms, one with access to the terrace
  • Modern bathroom with a walk-in shower
  • Additional guest toilet for convenience
  • Entrance hall with storage space
  • A spacious balcony with sea or courtyard views

These apartments are perfect for families seeking comfort and functionality near the sea while enjoying modern design and natural light.
