Three-Bedroom Apartment

Our spacious three-bedroom apartments are designed for families seeking maximum comfort, natural light, and elegant living near the sea. With a total area of 112 sq.m, each unit includes:

  • A large living room with a dining area and open-plan kitchen
  • Three bright bedrooms, with the master bedroom having access to the terrace
  • Two modern bathrooms with walk-in showers
  • Entrance hall with built-in wardrobe and storage space
  • A spacious balcony with sea or courtyard views

These apartments are ideal for larger families or those who desire more space while enjoying a modern and functional design in a peaceful environment.
