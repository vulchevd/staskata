Convenient Parking Solutions

Secure and accessible parking options for residents and guests, designed for your peace of mind.

Parking Information

At our residential complex, we understand the importance of convenient parking. That's why we offer a variety of options for both residents and their guests:

Underground Garages

We offer 28 secure, well-lit underground garages with easy access to all floors via elevator. Each garage provides protection from weather elements and enhanced security for your vehicle.

Outdoor Parking

Our property includes 43 spacious outdoor parking spaces, monitored by security cameras for extra safety. These spots are conveniently located near building entrances.

Visitor Parking

Special spots reserved for your guests, available with easy registration at the reception. Ensure your visitors always have a place to park when they come to see you.

Parking Assignment

All residents are assigned a designated parking space upon purchase of their apartment. Additional parking spaces are available for purchase or rent upon request, subject to availability.

Parking Size and Access

Each parking space measures approximately 2.5 meters wide by 5 meters long, designed to comfortably fit standard and large vehicles. Access to the parking area is secured by remote-controlled gates, and entry is available 24/7 for residents.

Need More Information?

For parking rates, availability, or to request an additional parking space, please contact our management office.
