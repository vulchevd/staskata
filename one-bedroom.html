One-Bedroom Apartment

Our one-bedroom apartments are designed for comfort, functionality, and modern living. With a total area of 58 sq.m, each unit includes:

  • A spacious living room with an open-plan kitchen area
  • One cozy bedroom with access to a terrace
  • Modern bathroom with walk-in shower
  • Entrance hall with a storage corner
  • A private balcony with views towards the courtyard or the sea

These apartments are ideal for singles or couples looking for a practical yet elegant home in a peaceful environment near the sea.
